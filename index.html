Pacific Crest Trail Overlaying Erwin Raisz's Classic U.S. Landform Map


Link to high resolution map

Authored by Tim Kohaya for University of Kentucky New Maps Plus Class 671

This map was created in 2018 and shows the route of the Pacific Crest Trail overlaying Erwin Raisz's classic U.S. Landforms Map. The U.S. Landforms map was georeferenced to a graticule shapefile through the GDAL Georeferencer Plugin in QGIS.

Data Sources:
USDA Forest Service https://www.fs.usda.gov/detail/pct/home/?cid=stelprdb5322473
Raisz Landform Maps http://www.raiszmaps.com

Coordinate Reference System (CRS): North America Albers Equal Area Conic (EPSG: 102008).
